Professor Kuah Kah Eng (visiting academic)

Kuah Kah Eng is a highly regarded scientist and academic with a substantial portfolio of achievements and distinction. He has taught Physics and Electrical Engineering at UC Berkeley and Tennessee in the United States. Prior to these positions, Kuah Kuah Eng spent a few years with NASA as a research scientist at Marshall Space Research Centre in the US.

Kuah received his Ph.D. from the University of California, Berkeley in Electrical Engineering, Master and Bachelor in Electrical Engineering from Tennessee State, Master in Business Administration in Entrepreneurship from University of Louisville, US, Master in Education from Monash University, Master in Organization Leadership from Monash, Australia. He also has a diploma in Human Resource Management, Chinese Language, Strategic Management, Business Management & Leadership, and Business Law.
